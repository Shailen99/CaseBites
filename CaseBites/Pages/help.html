Restaurant Manager Help Guide for CaseBites

If you're a restaurant manager using a CaseBites to manage your restaurant's orders, here's a guide to help you get started:

  1. Set Up Your Account

    2. The first step is to create an account with the CaseBites. You will need to provide your restaurant's information, such as your business name, address, and contact information. You may also need to provide your menu and pricing information, as well as your delivery zone and fees.

  2. Communicate with Customers

    3. You can use the CaseBites to communicate with customers about their experience and resolve any issues or complaints they may have.

  3. Adjust Your Menu and Pricing

    4. If you need to make changes to your menu or pricing, you can do so through the app. Make sure to keep your menu and pricing up-to-date to avoid any confusion or delays with orders.

  4. Manage Your Account and Payments

    5. You can manage your account and payments through the CaseBites's dashboard. You can view reviews to help you track your restaurant's performance.

Using CaseBites can be a convenient way to manage your restaurant's orders and increase your visibility to potential customers. Give it a try and see how it works for your restaurant!
